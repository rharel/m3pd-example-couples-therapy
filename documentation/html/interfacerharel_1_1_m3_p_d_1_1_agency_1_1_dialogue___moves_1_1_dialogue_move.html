Detailed Description

Represents a communicated meaning.

Dialogue moves are a way to refer to the act of communicating meanings through behavior, independent of the concrete form(s) such behavior may take.

To illustrate: both a hand-wave as well as a verbal "hello" may be regarded as concrete forms of the same move: a greeting. The level of abstraction desired is dependent on the use-case.

DialogueMove<T> for the generic version.
